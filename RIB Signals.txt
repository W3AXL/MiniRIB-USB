Serial Outputs
---------------------------------------------
Serial TX High     -> 	BUS+ 0V, BUS- 5V
Serial TX Low      -> 	BUS+ 5V, BUS- Floating

Serial DTR High    ->	~BUSY 5V, BUSY 0V, CTS/RI +5V
Serial DTR Low     ->	~BUSY 0V, BUSY 5V, CTS/RI -5V

Serial Inputs
---------------------------------------------
BUS+ 0V, BUS- 5V   ->	Serial RX High
BUS+ 5V, BUS- 0V   ->	Serial RX Low

~BUSY 0V, BUSY 5V  ->	CTS/RI -5V
~BUSY 5V, BUSY 0V  ->	CTS/RI +5V
